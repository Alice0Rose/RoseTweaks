[h1]Features[/h1]
Allows a total of 12 tradition slots and 15 ascension perks (2 obtained through a repeatable society tech)! \o/ The planetary ascension maximum is also increased to 17 to match the ascension perk increase, though the effects and costs of planetary ascension are scaled to make max ascension match vanilla max ascension :)

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
This mod overwrites a few defines, a scripted variable, and the tradition interface ui, which are listed below. There shouldn't be any issue with other mods unless they add tradition slots or ascension perk slots :)

common\defines\00_defines.txt
- ASCENSION_PERKS_SLOTS
- PLANET_ASCENSION_HARD_CAP
- PLANET_ASCENSION_SPRAWL_SCALE
- PLANET_ASCENSION_SUM_SCALE
- PLANET_ASCENSION_SPRAWL_MODIFIER
- PLANET_ASCENSION_MODIFIER_SCALE
- TRADITION_COST_TRADITION
- TRADITION_COST_TRADITION_EXP
- TRADITION_COST_MULT_TRADITION_GROUP
- TRADITION_CATEGORIES_MAX
- NUM_TRADITIONS_FOR_EDICTS

common\scripted_variables\07_scripted_variables_machine_age.txt
- max_tradition_trees

interface\topbar_traditions_view.gui
- FULL OVERRIDE
