[h1]Features[/h1]
This mod reworks the Juggernaut by greatly enhancing its combat capabilities while retaining its shipyard functionality.

The following changes have been made to the Juggernaut:
- Given 3 T slots and 2 modular sections that can be set as Carrier or Artillery sections, giving the Juggernaut orders of magnitude more firepower than any other ship
- Given more defensive utility slots
- Given a +40% multiplier to armor and shield health and a +20% multiplier to weapon damage
- Has 3 instead of 4 Aux slots

If you want more combat improvements and the ability to add the Juggernaut to fleets with other ships at the cost of shipyard functionality, use the following mod instead:[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3508826866] Juggernaut Rework (Combat) [/url]

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
This mod only overwrites the Juggernaut ship size. This mean's it's pretty unlikely it'll cause issues with any other mods unless they add to or modify Juggernaut functionality! ^-^
