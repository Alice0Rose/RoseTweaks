[h1]Features[/h1]
This mod fixes the issue of wayyyy too many virtual pops spawning on your colonies (especially noticeable with arkships after v4.4) by ethically removing all the extra pops :)

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
This mod only overrides the cybernetics.2027 event in machine_age_events_2, which handles creating and destroying virtual pops, so its unlikely to have many compatibility issues with other mods.
