[h1]Features[/h1]
This mod prevent behemoths from raging when they run into other behemoths. This means that so long as you keep your behemoths fed/fully grown, they you can safely have a whole heard of them!!

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
This mod only overrides 1 vanilla event, which means it should be compatible with any mods that don't specifically touch this event, and don't add some other event to trigger behemoth rage :)

events\biogenesis_crisis_events.txt
- biocrisis.90
